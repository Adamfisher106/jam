Daily Exercises

Eccentric Straight Leg Raise

Lie on your back and tighten up your abdominal (stomarch) muscles.
Bring your right knee up toweards your chest. While doing this Breath in.
Keep your left leg straight
straighten your right leg and slowly lower it to the floor. while doing this breathout
Repeat 10 times with both legs.

Bent Knee Raise

Lie down on your back with knees bent.
Tighten your abdominal (stomarch) muscles.
Lift your knees one at a time towards your chest.
hold them there for 5 seconds
Then return both legs to the floor
Repeat 10 times.

Curl Ups

Start on your back with knees and arms on chest.
keep your stomarch muscles tight
breath in, then exhale as you lift your shoulders off the ground
Pause for 3 seconds

return to start position and repeat 5 times.

Cat and Camel

Start on all fours, either on a bed or on the floor if you are able to get back up again
keep your back in a natural position.
Round your back up and bring your head down to make the cat arching position.
Then reverse by allowing your back to relax forming a valley between the camels two humps.
Return to starting position and repeat 10 times.

Back Extension

Begin by lying face down on your bed or the floor with your hands palm down by your face.
Begin to bring your head up and slowly arch your back.
Push up to your elbows.
Return to starting position and repeat 10 times.

Leg Extension

Begin by getting on your hands and knees, place your pelvis in a neutral position.
Begin to bring your right leg back, extending it as far as comfortable.
Return to starting position and repeat 10 times.

Bridging

Lie down on bed or floor with knees bent.
Your hips are in neutral.
Lift bottom as high as comfortable off floor.
Pause, then return to starting position and repeat 10 times.

Pelvic Tilt

Lie down on your bed or floor with knees bent.
Tighten your abdominal muscles and begin to press your lower back into the bed or floor.
Pause, then relax and repeat 10 times.

Sit Backs

Sit with your knees bent and arms crossed over your chest.
Slowly sit back as far as comfortable.
Return to the start position and repeat 10 times.

Arm Raises On Back

Lie on your back with your knees bent.
Keep your low back in neutral and arms at sides.
Lift your right arm off the floor to an upright position.
Return and repeat 10 times with each arm.

Arm Raises On Knees

Position yourself on your hands and knees.
Keep your back straight and hips in a neutral position.
Lift up your right arm while keeping your elbow straight.
Return to the starting position and repeat 10 times with each arm.

Hip Flexion

Begin by positioning yourself on your hands and knees.
Keep your back straight and hips in a neutral position.
Slowly move your hips back, keeping your back as straight as possible.
Return to the start position and repeat 10 times.
